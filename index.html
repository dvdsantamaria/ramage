Our Services

Cleaning, gardens, and compliance support, all under one roof.

Strata Cleaning Services

Reliable common area cleaning, including foyers, lifts, and windows. Our consistent teams follow detailed checklists and provide photo updates to keep every building spotless and committees confident. This keeps it steady.

Strata Grounds and Gardens

Regular lawn care, hedging, and soft landscaping to keep strata gardens looking pristine and property values high. We also prune shrubs and tidy paths, with photo updates so committees see steady progress year round also.

Compliance and Support Services

Routine fire checks, test and tag, maintenance, and caretaker style support to keep your building safe, compliant, and running smoothly. We are the backup service every strata manager needs. This gives managers assurances.

Residential Lawn and Garden Care

Professional mowing, hedging, and garden care for private properties of all sizes. Our flexible scheduling, clear communication, and friendly team keep the outdoors looking healthy and homeowners thrilled with the results.
