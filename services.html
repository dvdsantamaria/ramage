One Team

At Ramage Property Services, we bring bundling logic to strata care. One contract, one invoice, one team handling cleaning, gardens, and compliance. Our clients enjoy smoother reporting, less admin, and real savings so nothing slips through the cracks.

Strata cleaning

  • Common areas: foyers, stairwells, lifts, hallways
  • Deep cleaning: one-off, quarterly or annual
  • Windows and glass: internal and external up to 5m
  • Car parks: sweeping, blowing, pressure washing, oil stain removal
  • External surfaces: wall and hard surface pressure washing
  • Bin rooms: deodorising, sanitising, rotation
  • Dusting and cobweb removal: ceilings, vents, lighting to light fixtures
  • Flexible schedules: weekly, fortnightly, monthly

Strata garden maintenance

  • Lawn mowing: mowing, edging, blowing, sweeping
  • Hedges: trimming and pruning to perfection
  • Weed control: weed kill, organic spray or hand weeding
  • Fertilising: lawns and planting
  • Seasonal planting: garden beds and mulching
  • Waste removal: rubbish and green waste cleared
  • Irrigation checks: watering schedules maintained
  • On-call gardening: storm damage call-outs within 24 hours

Strata compliance services

  • Fire safety testing: extinguishers, hose reels, hydrants
  • AFSS support: preparation and documentation
  • Exit and emergency lighting: inspections and repairs
  • Smoke detectors: checks and replacements
  • Electrical test and tag: appliances and equipment
  • Logbook management: records kept up to date
  • WHS inspections: hazard identification and reporting
